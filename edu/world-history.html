World History

Welcome to the World History course. In this course, you will explore the events, people, and movements that have shaped our world from ancient times to the present. By the end of this course, you will have a deeper understanding of how history influences our current society and the global community.

Course Outline

  • Introduction to World History
  • Ancient Civilizations
  • Medieval Period
  • Renaissance and Reformation
  • Colonialism and the Age of Exploration
  • Revolutions and the Modern Era
  • World Wars and the 20th Century
  • Contemporary History and Globalization
  • Project: Historical Analysis of a Major Event
