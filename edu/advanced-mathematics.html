Advanced Mathematics

Welcome to the Advanced Mathematics course. In this course, you will explore advanced topics in mathematics including calculus, algebra, and problem-solving techniques. By the end of this course, you will have a deep understanding of mathematical concepts and be able to apply them to solve complex problems.

Course Outline

  • Introduction to Advanced Mathematics
  • Calculus: Differentiation and Integration
  • Linear Algebra: Matrices and Vectors
  • Abstract Algebra: Groups, Rings, and Fields
  • Number Theory
  • Combinatorics and Graph Theory
  • Probability and Statistics
  • Advanced Problem-Solving Techniques
  • Project: Mathematical Modelling
